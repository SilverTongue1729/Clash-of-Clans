## Controls

### Hero &#129464;

- w - move up
- a - move left
- d - move right
- s - move down
- 1 - Special Attack
- space - Normal Attack

### Barbarian &#128481;

- z - spawn at point 1
- x - spawn at point 2
- c - spawn at point 3

### Dragon &#128009;

- v - spawn at point 1
- b - spawn at point 2
- n - spawn at point 3

### Archer &#127993;

- i - spawn at point 1
- o - spawn at point 2
- p - spawn at point 3

### Balloon &#127880;

- j - spawn at point 1
- k - spawn at point 2
- l - spawn at point 3

### Sneaky Archers &#127993;

- f - spawn at point 1
- g - spawn at point 2
- h - spawn at point 3

### Healers &#9877;

- t - spawn at point 1
- y - spawn at point 2
- u - spawn at point 3

q - Quit Game
